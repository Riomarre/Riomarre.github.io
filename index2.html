Who are we?

So, who are we?
We are a group of students/workers whose job is to help the international community become part of the local! So instead of exploring Torino as a toursit, you get explore it as a local. You get all the cool secret locations and explore the city from a completely uniquie perspective.

More about us

AEGEE-Torino is a voluntary association founded in 1992 by a group of students from Torino. It is one of the local branches of AEGEE-Europe, a European network of interdisciplinary student associations with more than 15,000 members and 217 locations across Europe.
Locally, AEGEE-Torino is committed to spread the key values of the network, like Sustainability, Gender Equality and European Citizenship.

To achieve our objectives, we organize conferences, courses, round tables, research and other events on European social issues such as multilingualism, enlargement and diversity. Over the past 4 years more than 6000 young people have participated in 40 different local and international projects. All our projects focus on integrating the educational tools of formal education with non-formal and informal educational activities. The members of AEGEE-Torino are all young people between 18 and 35 years old who actively cooperate for the realization of the main projects.

Members of our NGO unlock exclusive offers including affordable exchanges with other antennas.
